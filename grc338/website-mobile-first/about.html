Our Beginning

It all started when a sleep-deprived college student needed caffeine for her late night study session with friends. She realized that there weren't any coffee shops around the area that offered unique drinks that she wanted. So she thought to herself, why not start my own café? Lo and behold, Le Rêve Café was born.
