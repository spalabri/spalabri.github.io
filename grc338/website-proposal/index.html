Introduction

Dang Bang Records

Dang Bang Records is a new and used record store located in San Luis Obispo, CA. As one of the largest vinyl retailers, we make it easy for people to find records they love and enjoy. Browse through our amazing selection and find records by category, artist, collection, and more.

Target audience

The target audience for this website are music lovers and record collectors.

Their primary tasks are to make a record purchase and to browse through the selection of records available. Their goal is to be able to visit the website and buy records or other related merchandise. They also want to have a smooth and efficient online buying experience.

FAQ

  1. When will my order be shipped?

    All orders will be shipped within 48 hours of receipt of payment. Preorders will be shipped as soon as possible upon receipt to us to try to make sure you get as close to release date as possible. Any items bought with a preorder will be held until the preorder arrives.

  2. What forms of payment do you accept?

    We take Visa, Mastercard, and Paypal.

  3. Why was I refunded or partially refunded?

    Please feel free to contact us if this happens. Things go missing, get stolen, misfiled, etc.

  4. Do you buy used stuff?

    Totally, we are always looking for quality used LPs and CDs (and even turntables, receivers, and cassettes).

  5. What is your return policy?

    Every once in a while an album escapes the pressing plant a little less pristine than it should be. If this is the case, please contact us before returning the defective product, so that we can know to expect it and can be prepared with a new copy for you. Please note that defective product must be returned within 30 days from date of purchase and should have proof of purchase from Dang Bang Records.

    Returned product may be exchanged for the same item or returned for a refund. You, the customer, are initially responsible for the cost of shipping the item back to Dang Bang Records, but will be reimbursed for these charges once we have received the item back. If an item is purchased in our physical store, please note that it is returnable only for store credit or exchange for the same item, no cash refunds.
